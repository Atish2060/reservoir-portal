Our Mission

Water Resource Management

Ensure sustainable and equitable management of Nepal's water resources for present and future generations.

  • Develop integrated water resource management plans
  • Promote rainwater harvesting and groundwater recharge
  • Protect watersheds and river ecosystems

Hydropower Development

Harness Nepal's hydropower potential to drive economic growth and energy independence.

  • Develop large, medium, and small-scale hydropower projects
  • Promote public-private partnerships in energy development
  • Ensure environmental sustainability in hydropower projects

Irrigation Infrastructure

Enhance agricultural productivity through modern and efficient irrigation systems.

  • Expand irrigation coverage to remote areas
  • Modernize existing irrigation infrastructure
  • Promote water-efficient farming practices

Flood Control & Disaster Management

Mitigate flood risks and protect communities from water-related disasters.

  • Develop flood forecasting and early warning systems
  • Construct flood control infrastructure
  • Promote community-based disaster preparedness

Ecosystem Protection

Preserve and restore water-related ecosystems for biodiversity and sustainability.

  • Protect wetlands, rivers, and lakes
  • Promote afforestation in watershed areas
  • Combat water pollution and promote clean water initiatives

Public Awareness & Education

Empower citizens through education and awareness about water conservation.

  • Conduct nationwide water conservation campaigns
  • Develop educational programs for schools and communities
  • Promote water-saving technologies and practices
