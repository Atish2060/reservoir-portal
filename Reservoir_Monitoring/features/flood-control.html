Flood Control and Disaster Management

Flood control is a top priority for the Government of Nepal, particularly in the Terai region, which is highly vulnerable to monsoon floods. Our initiatives focus on protecting lives, property, and infrastructure.

Key Measures

  • Embankments: Construction of 100 km of new embankments
  • Early Warning Systems: Installation of advanced flood forecasting technology
  • Community Training: Programs to educate residents on flood preparedness

Impact on Communities

These measures have:

  • Reduced flood-related deaths by 50% in the last 5 years
  • Protected over 1 million people from flood damage
  • Improved agricultural productivity in flood-prone areas

Future Plans

The government plans to invest $200 million in flood control infrastructure over the next decade, with a focus on sustainable and community-driven solutions.
