Rasuwagadi Hydropower Project

About Rasuwagadi Hydropower

The Rasuwagadi Hydropower Project is a run-of-river project located in the Rasuwa District of Nepal. This project utilizes the water flow of the Trishuli River to generate clean and sustainable electricity.

With an installed capacity of 111 MW, Rasuwagadi is one of Nepal’s key hydropower projects, contributing significantly to the country's electricity production and energy security.

The project features a weir, headrace tunnel, surge shaft, and powerhouse to ensure efficient power generation throughout the year. It plays a crucial role in reducing Nepal's dependency on imported electricity.
