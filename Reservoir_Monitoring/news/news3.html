Nationwide Water Conservation Campaign Launched

Published on: October 1, 2023

The Government of Nepal has launched a nationwide water conservation campaign to promote sustainable water usage. The campaign aims to educate citizens about water-saving practices, rainwater harvesting, and efficient irrigation techniques.

Campaign Objectives

  • Reduce water wastage by 20% over the next 5 years
  • Promote rainwater harvesting in urban and rural areas
  • Encourage the use of water-efficient irrigation systems

Key Initiatives

  • Public Awareness Programs: Workshops and seminars in schools and communities
  • Subsidies for Rainwater Harvesting: Financial incentives for households and businesses
  • Partnerships with NGOs: Collaboration with local and international organizations

Expected Outcomes

The campaign is expected to benefit over 5 million people across Nepal, with a focus on improving water availability and reducing the impact of droughts.
