New Hydropower Project Launched in Gandaki Region

Published on: October 10, 2023

The Government of Nepal has officially launched a new hydropower project in the Gandaki region, which is expected to generate 500 MW of electricity. This project is part of the government's broader initiative to address the growing energy demands of the region and promote sustainable development.

Project Highlights

  • Capacity: 500 MW
  • Location: Gandaki River Basin
  • Investment: $500 million
  • Completion Date: 2026

Environmental Impact

The project has been designed with a strong focus on environmental sustainability. Measures include:

  • Use of eco-friendly construction practices
  • Minimizing deforestation and land degradation
  • Implementing fish ladders to protect aquatic life

Community Benefits

The project will bring significant benefits to local communities, including:

  • Creation of over 1,000 jobs during construction
  • Improved access to electricity for remote villages
  • Funding for local schools and healthcare facilities
